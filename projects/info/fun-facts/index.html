Ten Fun Facts!

  1. Having a too high blood pressure can permanently damage your eyes! (WebMD)
  2. Having a high sodium diet has many benefits, such as water retention, but it can contribute to high blood pressure by cotributing to a hypertonic solution! (Helm)
  3. Our modern way of life contributes to many factors that can cause hypertension! (S., Kulkarni et al.)
  4. If you follow a stereotypically healthy lifestyle (no drugs, no alcohol, plenty of exercise, good food), you cut out most of the risks of hypertension!(Virdis et al.; Kazim et al.; Pescatello et al.)
  5. Short periods of hypertension, similar to fevers, can actually be beneficial for healing! (Shiozaki)
  6. Sleep loss can be a contributor to hypertension! (Palagini)
  7. Many medical problems, including hypertension, can be excacerbated in areas with decreased general awareness! (Xu)
  8. Rural China is currently undergoing a hypertensive epidemic! (Xu)
  9. Hypertension can sometimes be induced from pregnancy! (WebMD)
  10. Hypertension is often an indicator of other problems! It is very rarely completely isolated from other conditions. (Pescatello et al.; Palagini et al.; Kulkarni et al.; Grollman; Virdis et al.; Husain et al.; Shiozaki; WebMD;)
