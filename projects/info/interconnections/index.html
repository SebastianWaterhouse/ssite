Interconnections!

Tobacco use:

Hypertensive tobacco users have a much higher chance of developing worse forms of hypertension. Not smoking is one of the best ways to prevent heart disease such as hypertension

Alcohol use:

There is an undeniable link between alcohol use and hypertension. The most effective prevention of hypertension caused by alcohol, save for cessation of alcohol use, is exercise

Physical damage:

Hypertension can often be found in the acute phase of head injury. Hypertension may be beneficial to the healing of a head injury

Unhealthy diet:

Low sodium diets can cause hypotension. While a high-sodium diet can relieve hypotension, it may also cause hypertension.

Lack of physical activity:

Exercise programs with more cardiovascular work can prevent hypertension. Exercise is a great prevention and cure for hypertension.
